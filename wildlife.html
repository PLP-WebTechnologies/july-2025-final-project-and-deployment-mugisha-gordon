Wildlife Conservation

Protecting Africa's Natural Heritage

Africa is home to some of the world's most iconic wildlife, but many species face threats from habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict. Conservation efforts across the continent are working to protect these magnificent animals for future generations.

Organizations and governments are implementing strategies such as anti-poaching patrols, community-based conservation, habitat restoration, and wildlife corridors to ensure the survival of Africa's diverse species.
